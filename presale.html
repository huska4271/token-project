NOBAL Presale Tutorial

Welcome to NOBAL Presale

This tutorial will guide you through the process of purchasing NOB tokens during the presale phase. You'll learn how to connect your wallet, check your balance, and complete your purchase.

The tutorial has 6 steps and will take about 3 minutes to complete.

Customize Your Experience

Use the theme toggle button to switch between light and dark modes based on your preference.

The interface will remember your choice for future visits.

Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button to link your MetaMask wallet to the application.

Make sure you're connected to the Base Network for successful transactions.

Check Your Network

The network indicator shows whether you're connected to the correct network (Base Network).

If it shows "Wrong Network", the application will guide you through switching to the correct one.

Making a Purchase

Enter the amount of ETH you want to spend in the "You Pay" field. The "You Receive" field will automatically update with the equivalent NOB amount.

The current exchange rate is 1 ETH = 2000 NOB tokens.

Transaction Process

The progress indicator shows the four steps of the purchase process:

  • Connect Wallet
  • Approve Transaction
  • Purchase Tokens
  • Complete Transaction

You can track your progress through these steps during your purchase.

You're All Set!

You've completed the tutorial and are ready to purchase NOB tokens.

If you need to review this tutorial again, click the question mark button in the bottom right corner.

Thank you for participating in the NOBAL presale!

Purchase Successful!

Your NOB tokens have been purchased successfully. The tokens will be sent to your wallet shortly.
